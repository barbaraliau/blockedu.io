What is BlockEDU?

BlockEDU is an alternative education platform where Distributed Open Collaborative Courses (DOCC), Small Private Online Course (SPOC), and institution free traditional Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) or online universites can be explored. The goal of BlockEDU is to make quality and reputable education affordable for all. BlockEDU will offer the ability for anyone to create custom courses or structure degree programs that conform to current academic paths.
